## Live Links

- **Presentation:** https://vonwao.github.io/skillbites-pitch/

## How to Present

1. Open the presentation URL on your laptop
2. Press **F11** (or Cmd+Ctrl+F on Mac) for full screen
3. Open notes.html on your phone for speaker notes

### Navigation
- **Arrow keys** ← → or **Spacebar** to navigate slides
- **Click** left/right side of screen
- **N** = Toggle speaker notes overlay
- **M** = Notes-only mode (all notes on one page)
