# SkillBites Pitch Deck

## How to Present

1. Open `pitch-deck.html` in any browser
2. Press **F11** (or Cmd+Shift+F on Mac) for full screen
3. Navigate with:
   - **Arrow keys** ← →
   - **Spacebar** (next)
   - **Click** left/right side of screen

## Slides Overview

| # | Title | Duration | Notes |
|---|-------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Title | 15s | Hook them with tagline |
| 2 | The Problem | 45s | Relatable school vs life |
| 3 | The Gap | 30s | Existing solutions suck |
| 4 | The Solution | 60s | SkillBites overview |
| 5 | Course Library | 45s | Show breadth |
| 6 | Sample Courses | 30s | Concrete examples |
| 7 | Target Market | 45s | Who buys this |
| 8 | Business Model | 30s | Simple pricing |
| 9 | Competitive Advantage | 45s | Why we win |
| 10 | Traction | 30s | What's built |
| 11 | Vision | 45s | Where we're going |
| 12 | CTA | 15s | skillbites.xyz |

**Total: ~8-10 minutes** (leaves room for Q&A)

## Customization

Edit `pitch-deck.html` directly. Key sections:
- Stats (316+ courses, $4.99, etc.)
- Course examples (slide 6)
- Vision timeline (slide 11)
- Contact info (slide 12)

## Tips

- **For investors:** Emphasize scalable AI pipeline, B2B potential
- **For users:** Demo the actual site, show course flow
- **For partners:** Focus on content breadth, integration opportunities
